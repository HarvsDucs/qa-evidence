GRADE 7 MATHEMATICS — WORKSHEET: EXPONENTS, FRACTIONS, AND EQUATIONS

A. LAWS OF EXPONENTS

The area of a square with side s is s². The volume of a cube is s³.
Recall: a² × a³ = a⁵ (add the exponents), and (a²)³ = a⁶ (multiply them).
Scientific notation: the speed of light is about 3.0 × 10⁸ m/s, and one mole
contains 6.02 × 10²³ particles.

1. Simplify: 2⁴ × 2³ = ?
2. Write 45,000,000 in scientific notation.
3. If s = 5 cm, find the volume s³ of the cube.

B. FRACTIONS AND DECIMALS

A recipe uses ½ cup of sugar and ¾ cup of flour. Ana only wants to make
half the recipe, so she needs ¼ cup of sugar and ⅜ cup of flour.

4. Compute: ½ + ⅓ = ? (Express as a single fraction: 5/6.)
5. Which is larger, ⅝ or 0.6? Show your comparison.
6. Simplify the fraction 18/24 to lowest terms.
